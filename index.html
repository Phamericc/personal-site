About Me

Where osteopathic medicine
meets storytelling.

I'm Eric Pham — a DO student at UIW School of Osteopathic Medicine in San Antonio, TX. I chose osteopathic medicine because it treats the whole person, not just the diagnosis. That philosophy shapes everything I do.

Outside the lecture hall and anatomy lab, I create content, build ideas, and stay deeply curious about the world beyond medicine. I believe the best future physicians are the ones who never stop learning — in all directions.
